Mortgage & finance FAQs

Does ChatGPT recommend mortgage brokers in New Zealand?

Yes. When someone asks an assistant like ChatGPT or Google's AI Mode for a mortgage broker in an NZ town, it returns a short list of names rather than a page of links. In a logged-out test for a broker in Orewa, both ChatGPT and Google AI Mode named a local adviser we work with alongside established firms. Which brokers get named depends on how clearly and consistently they appear across the web.

Why does AI name another broker and not me?

AI builds answers from the sources it can read and the businesses it sees described consistently across the web — your site, reviews, directories, forums. If your site is hard for AI to read, or you're described inconsistently or barely mentioned beyond your own pages, the assistant fills the gap with whoever's clearer. It's rarely about who's the better adviser.

Is this financial advice?

No. Eko Media provides AI-visibility and marketing services only. We don't give financial or legal advice and we don't assess regulated advice. We make your existing, factual information easier for AI to find, trust and quote.

Will this comply with FMA rules?

We keep all client-facing content factual and recommend your compliance adviser reviews anything before it's published. We handle visibility; you and your compliance adviser remain responsible for the regulated content. We won't publish advice or product claims on your behalf without that review.

How long until AI starts naming me?

It varies, and it isn't guaranteed. It depends on how legible your site is, how consistently you're mentioned elsewhere, and how each assistant updates. We've seen movement within weeks on some engines for clients we've built for — but no one controls how or when a model phrases an answer.

Can you guarantee I'll be the top recommendation?

No. No one controls how an AI model phrases its answer, and anyone promising a guaranteed number-one mention is bluffing. What we control is whether you're readable, trustworthy and present in the sources AI draws from. That's where citations come from.
